Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

HesGoal Live ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our sports streaming platform, including any related services, features, or content we provide.

By using HesGoal Live, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with the practices described, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit HesGoal Live, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage patterns:

  • IP address and location data
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information (operating system, screen resolution)
  • Pages visited and time spent on our platform
  • Referral sources and search terms
  • Stream preferences and viewing history

2.2 Information You Provide

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide when:

  • Using our chat features (Chatango integration)
  • Submitting feedback or contacting us
  • Participating in community discussions

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide access to live sports streams, match schedules, and related content
  • Platform Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and enhance user experience
  • Content Personalization: To recommend relevant sports content based on your preferences
  • Technical Support: To troubleshoot issues and maintain platform functionality
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and respond to legal requests
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Streaming Services

HesGoal Live aggregates links to external streaming services. We do not host or control the content on these third-party platforms. When you click on a stream link, you are redirected to external websites that have their own privacy policies.

4.2 Chatango Chat Service

Our platform integrates Chatango for live chat functionality. Chatango may collect and process information according to their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review Chatango's privacy practices.

4.3 Advertising Partners

We display advertisements from third-party ad networks. These partners may use cookies and similar technologies to deliver personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your browser settings.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic platform functionality
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your theme settings (dark/light mode) and viewing preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our platform
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by ad networks to deliver relevant advertisements

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We do not store sensitive personal information such as payment details, as we do not process payments directly.

7. DMCA Compliance

HesGoal Live respects intellectual property rights and complies with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). We do not host copyrighted content directly. All streams are provided by third-party sources.

If you believe your copyrighted content has been infringed, please contact us with:

  • Identification of the copyrighted work
  • Location of the allegedly infringing material
  • Your contact information
  • A statement of good faith belief

8. Children's Privacy

HesGoal Live is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Users

HesGoal Live may be accessed from various countries. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your information
  • Objection to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdrawal of consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of HesGoal Live after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
